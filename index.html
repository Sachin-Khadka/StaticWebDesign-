History

Founded in 1996, the LTC (Language Teaching Centre) is an international language teaching Institute in Kathmandu Town, Nepal. In 2001, the business was taken over by Mechtild Braxmaier, a German entrepreneur who is passionate about education. The business is currently managed by Sachin Khadka. We offer English,Korean, Japenese and other language courses, training and certification in an encouraging, safe and positive educational setting. Teachers are highly qualified, native speakers of the languages they teach. They must have a TEFL, CELTA and/or a University English teaching degree. They have extensive experience in teaching students of many different nationalities.

Purpose

Our purpose is to connect people from various cultures around the world and to provide them with a space to grow. We do this by providing high quality English, Korean, Japanese and other language courses, training and certification in a personalised and encouraging learning environment.
