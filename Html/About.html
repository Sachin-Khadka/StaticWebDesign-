Welcome to LTC: Language Teaching Centre

Our languages Institute, based on the South coast of England, provides Languages lessons that are designed to increase knowledge, fluency, understanding and ability. We offer these Languages lessons to adults and groups throughout the year, and also to young learners during the summer. Our aim is simple. We want to ensure that our students have an unforgettable experience while studying languages with us. So what else makes LTC special, and why should students choose us?

Susmita Rae

CEO of Language Teaching Center.

My name is Susmita and CEO of TLC. I have been a language practitioner and a teacher for more than 23 years now. I have taught language in different schools and companies, from corporates to government organizations and NGOs. I did Linguistics with Unisa, Terminology and Language Planning with University of Stellenbosch. I am a translator, Interpreter and editor as well. I enjoy working with LTC and giving all my students the best education they can receive.
