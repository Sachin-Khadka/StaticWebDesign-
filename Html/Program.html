Korean Language

English Language

IELTS Test Module and Associated Training

IELTS has two different kinds of test, General Training module and Academic module. Students who are willing to go abroad for further studies must give an academic module exam whereas if you want to migrate to other countries or visit for non-study purposes you need to give general training IELTS module. We provide training to cover both modules for individuals wishing to apply for abroad for different purposes.

IELTS Test Format and Time Duration

IELTS test is categorized into four sub-sections: speaking, reading, writing and listening. The student must take tests for all four sections to complete their IELTS exam. This is applicable to both academic modules or general training module. The total duration of IELTS exam is around 2 hours and 45 minutes. The total time is divided among various sections. Listening section has 40 questions and the recording is played for 30 minutes. 10 minutes is provided to transfer the answer from the rough paper to actual answer sheet. Reading section is about 60 minutes where you are given 3 passages to answer 40 questions. In the writing section, you will need to write two essays within 60 minutes time duration.
